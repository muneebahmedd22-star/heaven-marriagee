Frequently Asked Questions

How do you verify the credentials of candidates? +

Our dedicated matchmakers perform physical, educational, and family background verifications. We speak directly with references and require genuine documents to maintain absolute trust in our bureau circles.

Is my data kept confidential? +

Yes, 100%. We operate with high-level privacy. Candidate photos and full contact coordinates are never shared publicly. We only share match criteria and seek parental approvals before introducing families.

What areas and cities do you cover? +

We serve families in Lahore, Karachi, Islamabad, KPK, Kashmir, South Punjab, other major cities of Pakistan, as well as Overseas families residing in the UK, USA, Canada, UAE, and Europe.

Do you arrange matches for overseas Pakistanis? +

Yes, we have a specialized database for international families. We understand the unique educational, visa, and cultural expectations of overseas Pakistani grooms and brides.
